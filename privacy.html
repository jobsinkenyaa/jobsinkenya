Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Welcome to Jobs in Kenya ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit jobsinkenya.it.com.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Email address (when you subscribe to job alerts)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time on site) via Google Analytics
  • Device and browser information automatically collected by cookies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send you daily job alert emails (only if you subscribed)
  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • Analyse website traffic and usage patterns

3. Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this and/or other sites.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device. We use cookies for:

  • Storing your job search preferences
  • Google Analytics tracking
  • Google AdSense advertising

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features of the website.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to employer websites and job boards. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before applying.

6. Email Subscriptions

If you subscribe to our job alerts, your email is stored securely via Mailchimp. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link in any email we send.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
